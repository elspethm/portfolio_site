Microsite + Banner Ad for Music Festival

www.google.com / December 2016

This is a microsite for an imagined music festival, sponsored by the music streaming service Tidal. I wanted to promote my ideal music festival featuring my favorite artists. The celestial concept behind bringing together music's "5 brightest stars," was inspired by the constellation Perseus, which contains the 5 brightest stars seen from Earth, and is featured in both the microsite and gif background. The microsite was 2 pages, the second page including more information about the festival. For this project, I also created an accompanying banner ad. [Disclaimer: I used the TIDAL logo purely for educational purposes as this was a class project.]
