Concert Poster

I designed a concert poster for New Orleans jazz musician Trombone Shorty, ideally as a magazine advertisement. This was an assignment for my Photoshop class, where I incorporated gradients, layer masks, and transparent blocks of color to organize this composition.

This stair-wrap image is to help envision what this poster would look like in a large scale context and in pieces, while still maintaining a visually coherent image.
